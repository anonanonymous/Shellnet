How to use your Keys

Import your wallet in zedwallet

  1. In the running zedwallet client, type I.

  2. Copy and paste your private spend and view key (Shown above).

  3. Set a name for your new wallet.

  4. Specify a strong password to encrypt your wallet with and confirm it by pressing Enter.

  5. Once done, press Enter. The wallet will then begin synchronizing with the blockchain. Once synchronization is complete, your balance will be updated.
